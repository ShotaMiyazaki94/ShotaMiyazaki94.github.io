About

I am Shota Miyazaki, a postdoctoral researcher at Osaka University. I belong to the MOA and PRIME collaborations that mainly study exoplanetary science using the gravitational microlensing method.

Work & Interests

I have been contributing to the study of exoplanetary demographics by analyzing planetary microlensing events, opening up new feasibilities of gravitational microlensing, and developing a new telescope for searching for microlensing exoplanets. Now, I am particularly interested in how environmental conditions affect planetary formations and evolutions. I would like to reveal Earth's context in our Galaxy.
